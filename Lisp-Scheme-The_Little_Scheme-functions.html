《the little scheme》functions

lat?

(lat? l) is just another way to ask "Whether every S-expression is l is an atom?".

(define lat?
  (lambda (l)
    (cond
     ((null? l) #t)
     ((atom? (car l)) (lat? (cdr l)))
     (else #f))))
member?

(member? a lat) is just another way to ask "Is a is one of the atoms of the lat".

(define member?
  (lambda (a lat)
    (cond
     ((null? lat) #f)
     (else (or (eq? (car lat) a)
               (member? a (cdr lat)))))))
